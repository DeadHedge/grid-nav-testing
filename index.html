A website about squid

Squid are really the best. They like to swim a lot and they feel real slimy. If you don't believe me, you should try to pick one up sometime. If you try to pick them up and they don't want to be picked up, there's really no way you can argue with them; they just slide right out. Now cuttlefish, that's a different matter. They're not nearly as slippery, but they're definitely a lot fightier.
